# 🤖 AI-Powered Marketing: Machine Learning & Generative AI

This repository contains the full syllabus, course materials, and project guidelines for the **AI-Powered Marketing** course.

**Duration:** 30 Hours (10 Sessions × 3 Hours)  
**Level:** Beginner – Intermediate  

---

## 📘 Course Description

This comprehensive 30-hour training program empowers marketing professionals and businesses to seamlessly integrate advanced machine learning and generative AI into their daily operations. Emphasizing human augmentation over total automation, the course is designed to position AI as a strategic co-pilot that enhances your team's creativity and analytical capabilities. Participants will learn to leverage data-driven insights, craft personalized customer experiences, and build practical AI tools—fostering a powerful synergy between human marketing expertise and cutting-edge technology.

---

## 👥 Audience

- Marketing professionals
- Business owners
- University students
- Vocational teachers
- Corporate innovation teams
- Digital agencies
- Startups

---

## 🎯 Learning Outcomes

After completing this course, participants will be able to:

- Understand Machine Learning concepts for marketing
- Analyze marketing data using Python
- Measure marketing KPIs using analytics
- Perform customer segmentation
- Build prediction models
- Perform sentiment analysis
- Create AI-powered marketing content
- Implement Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
- Develop an end-to-end AI marketing mini project

---

## 🗂️ Course Structure (10 Sessions)

### 🔹 Phase 1: Foundations (Data, KPIs & Predict)
| Session | Topic | Practical / Hands-on |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | **Intro to AI, ML & Marketing Analytics** <br> Evolution, Trends, Python Intro | Install Python, Anaconda, Colab, Jupyter |
| **2** | **Marketing KPIs & Data Analytics** <br> Funnels, Metrics (ROI, CAC, etc.), Viz | Visualizing KPI data with Python |
| **3** | **Predictive Marketing Analytics** <br> Data Cleaning, Regression, Decision Trees | Predict customer conversion using Python |

### 🔹 Phase 2: Insights (NLP, Segments & Recs)
| Session | Topic | Practical / Hands-on |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **4** | **Customer Segmentation (ML)** <br> Personas, RFM, K-Means Clustering | Segment customers into marketing groups |
| **5** | **Customer Insights & NLP** <br> Tokenization, Sentiment Analysis | Analyze customer reviews (Social Media, E-com) |
| **6** | **Recommendation Systems & Personalization** <br> Apriori, Collaborative Filtering | Build a simple product recommendation engine |

### 🔹 Phase 3: Gen AI (LLMs, RAG & Agents)
| Session | Topic | Practical / Hands-on |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **7** | **Generative AI for Marketing Content** <br> LLMs, Prompt Engineering, AI Copywriting | Create AI marketing campaigns using LLMs |
| **8** | **AI Agents & RAG** <br> Embeddings, LangChain, Vector Databases | Build a simple Marketing AI Assistant |
| **9** | **Future of AI Marketing & Governance** <br> Multimodal, Autonomous, Ethics, Hallucinations | Evaluate AI risks in marketing use cases |

### 🔹 Phase 4: Capstone (End-to-End Build)
| Session | Topic | Practical / Hands-on |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **10** | **Capstone Mini Project** | Develop a complete AI-powered marketing solution |

---

## 🎓 Session 10: Capstone Mini Project

Participants develop a complete AI-powered marketing solution. Choose **one** project:

### Option A: AI Marketing Dashboard
- Marketing KPIs
- Customer Segmentation
- Sentiment Analysis

### Option B: AI Content Generator
- Prompt Engineering
- Social Media Posts
- Email Marketing
- Product Description

### Option C: AI Customer Analytics
- Customer Prediction
- Churn Prediction
- Recommendation System

### ⭐️ Option D: AI Marketing Assistant (Recommended)
**Using:** Python, OpenAI/Gemini, LangChain, ChromaDB/FAISS, Streamlit/Gradio  
**Features:**
- Upload product catalog
- Ask marketing questions
- Generate campaigns
- Recommend products
- Analyze customer feedback
