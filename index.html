AI-Powered Marketing: Machine Learning & Generative AI

Duration: 30 Hours (10 Sessions × 3 Hours) | Level: Beginner – Intermediate

📘 Course Description

This comprehensive 30-hour training program empowers marketing professionals and businesses to seamlessly integrate advanced machine learning and generative AI into their daily operations. Emphasizing human augmentation over total automation, the course is designed to position AI as a strategic co-pilot that enhances your team's creativity and analytical capabilities. Participants will learn to leverage data-driven insights, craft personalized customer experiences, and build practical AI tools—fostering a powerful synergy between human marketing expertise and cutting-edge technology.
