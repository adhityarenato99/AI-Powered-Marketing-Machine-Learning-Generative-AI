# 🤖 AI-Powered Marketing: Machine Learning & Generative AI

This repository contains the mini syllabus, course structure, learning outcomes, teaching method, assessment plan, and project guidelines for the **AI-Powered Marketing: Machine Learning & Generative AI** course.

**Duration:** 30 Hours  
**Format:** 10 Sessions × 3 Hours  
**Level:** Beginner – Intermediate  
**Market Focus:** Indonesia  
**Audience:** Public learners, university students, marketing professionals, business owners, digital agencies, startups, vocational teachers, and corporate teams.

---

## 📘 Course Overview

This course introduces practical applications of **Machine Learning** and **Generative AI** for marketing. Participants learn how to read marketing data, measure campaign performance, segment customers, analyze customer sentiment, build recommendation systems, create AI-powered marketing content, and develop a simple Marketing AI Assistant using **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)**.

The course is designed for the Indonesian market, with examples from SMEs, digital agencies, e-commerce, social media marketing, customer service, corporate marketing teams, and public training contexts.

## 🎯 Learning Outcomes

After completing this course, participants will be able to:

- Understand AI, Machine Learning, and Generative AI concepts for marketing.
- Analyze marketing data using Python and simple visualization techniques.
- Measure marketing KPIs such as ROI, CPA, CAC, CLV, ROAS, and conversion rate.
- Perform customer segmentation and customer behavior prediction.
- Analyze customer sentiment from reviews, social media, and e-commerce scenarios.
- Build a basic product recommendation system.
- Create AI-powered marketing content using prompt engineering.
- Understand RAG, vector databases, and AI assistants for marketing knowledge bases.
- Develop a portfolio-ready AI marketing mini project.

## 🗂️ Course Structure

### 🔹 Phase 1: Foundations — Data, KPIs & Prediction

| Session | Topic | Practical / Hands-on |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | **Introduction to AI, Machine Learning & Marketing Analytics** <br> AI vs ML vs GenAI, marketing analytics overview, AI use cases in Indonesian business, Python environment. | Set up Python, Google Colab, Jupyter Notebook, and basic data workflow. |
| **2** | **Marketing KPIs & Data Analytics** <br> Marketing funnel, awareness, engagement, conversion, retention, ROI, CPA, CAC, CLV, ROAS, KPI dashboards. | Visualize KPI data using Python. |
| **3** | **Predictive Marketing Analytics** <br> Data cleaning, feature engineering, regression, decision tree, conversion prediction, and campaign response prediction. | Predict customer conversion using sample marketing data. |

---

### 🔹 Phase 2: Customer Insights — Segmentation, NLP & Recommendation

| Session | Topic | Practical / Hands-on |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **4** | **Customer Segmentation using Machine Learning** <br> Customer personas, RFM analysis, K-Means clustering, cluster interpretation, and campaign targeting. | Segment customers into meaningful marketing groups. |
| **5** | **Customer Insights with NLP & Sentiment Analysis** <br> Text cleaning, tokenization, sentiment analysis, topic discovery, customer review analysis, and brand monitoring. | Analyze sentiment from social media or e-commerce review data. |
| **6** | **Recommendation Systems & Marketing Personalization** <br> Market basket analysis, Apriori algorithm, association rules, collaborative filtering, and personalized offers. | Build a simple product recommendation engine. |

---

### 🔹 Phase 3: Generative AI — Content, RAG & Responsible Use

| Session | Topic | Practical / Hands-on |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **7** | **Generative AI for Marketing Content** <br> LLMs, prompt engineering, zero-shot, few-shot, content personalization, product descriptions, ads, email, and social media captions. | Create AI-powered marketing campaigns using LLM prompts. |
| **8** | **AI Agents & Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** <br> Embeddings, vector databases, LangChain, knowledge retrieval, AI assistant workflow, and marketing knowledge base. | Build a simple Marketing AI Assistant. |
| **9** | **Future of AI Marketing & AI Governance** <br> Multimodal AI, autonomous marketing, AR/VR marketing, AI ethics, privacy, bias, copyright, hallucination, and explainable AI. | Evaluate AI risks in marketing use cases. |

---

### 🔹 Phase 4: Capstone — End-to-End AI Marketing Solution

| Session | Topic | Practical / Hands-on |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **10** | **Final Capstone Mini Project** | Develop, test, document, and present a portfolio-ready AI-powered marketing solution. |

- Marketing KPI dashboard

- Sentiment analysis insights

- Prompt engineering workflow

**Features:**

- Upload product catalog or FAQ
- Ask marketing questions
- Generate campaign ideas
- Recommend products
- Analyze customer feedback

## 📊 Teaching Method

| Activity | Percentage | Description |
| :--- | :---: | :--- |
| Interactive Lecture | 30% | Concept explanation, real Indonesian marketing examples, and guided discussion. |
| Live Demonstration | 20% | Instructor demonstrates Python, ML workflow, prompt design, and RAG workflow. |
| Guided Practice | 25% | Participants follow structured exercises with instructor support. |
| Group Discussion | 10% | Use-case discussion, business interpretation, and peer feedback. |
| Hands-on Lab | 10% | Short technical labs for analytics, ML, NLP, GenAI, and RAG components. |
| **Project-Based Learning / Capstone Development** | **5%** | In-course project guidance and iterative development of the final capstone. |

> **Clarification:** The project item in the Teaching Method refers to the learning activity and development process. The Final Capstone Project in the Assessment section refers to the graded final deliverable.

## 🧮 Assessment

| Component | Weight | Description |
| :--- | :---: | :--- |
