AI-Powered Marketing:
Machine Learning & Generative AI

Mini syllabus for the Indonesian market: practical AI, analytics, machine learning, generative AI, and RAG for marketing teams, students, public learners, and companies.

📘 Course Overview

Duration 30 Hours
Format 10 Sessions × 3 Hours
Level Beginner – Intermediate
Market Focus Indonesia: public learners, students, SMEs, startups, digital agencies, and corporate teams

This course introduces practical applications of Machine Learning and Generative AI for marketing. Participants learn how to read marketing data, measure campaign performance, segment customers, analyze customer sentiment, build recommendation systems, create AI-powered marketing content, and develop a simple Marketing AI Assistant using RAG.

👥 Target Audience

Marketing Professionals Business Owners University Students Vocational Teachers Corporate Innovation Teams Digital Agencies Startups Public Learners

🎯 Learning Outcomes

Understand AI, Machine Learning, and Generative AI concepts for marketing.
Analyze marketing data using Python and simple visualization techniques.
Measure marketing KPIs such as ROI, CPA, CAC, CLV, and conversion rate.
Perform customer segmentation and customer behavior prediction.
Analyze customer sentiment from reviews, social media, and e-commerce scenarios.
Create AI-powered marketing content using prompt engineering.
Understand RAG, vector databases, and AI assistants for marketing knowledge bases.
Build a portfolio-ready AI marketing mini project.

🗂️ Course Structure

Session Topic Practical / Hands-on
Phase 1: Foundations — Data, KPIs & Prediction
1 Introduction to AI, Machine Learning & Marketing Analytics
AI vs ML vs GenAI, marketing analytics overview, AI use cases in Indonesian business, Python environment.		 Set up Python, Google Colab, Jupyter Notebook, and basic data workflow.
2 Marketing KPIs & Data Analytics
Marketing funnel, awareness, engagement, conversion, retention, ROI, CPA, CAC, CLV, ROAS, KPI dashboards.		 Visualize KPI data using Python.
3 Predictive Marketing Analytics
Data cleaning, feature engineering, regression, decision tree, conversion prediction, and campaign response prediction.		 Predict customer conversion using sample marketing data.
Phase 2: Customer Insights — Segmentation, NLP & Recommendation
4 Customer Segmentation using Machine Learning
Customer personas, RFM analysis, K-Means clustering, cluster interpretation, and campaign targeting.		 Segment customers into meaningful marketing groups.
5 Customer Insights with NLP & Sentiment Analysis
Text cleaning, tokenization, sentiment analysis, topic discovery, customer review analysis, and brand monitoring.		 Analyze sentiment from social media or e-commerce review data.
6 Recommendation Systems & Marketing Personalization
Market basket analysis, Apriori algorithm, association rules, collaborative filtering, and personalized offers.		 Build a simple product recommendation engine.
Phase 3: Generative AI — Content, RAG & Responsible Use
7 Generative AI for Marketing Content
LLMs, prompt engineering, zero-shot, few-shot, content personalization, product descriptions, ads, email, and social media captions.		 Create AI-powered marketing campaigns using LLM prompts.
8 AI Agents & Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
Embeddings, vector databases, LangChain, knowledge retrieval, AI assistant workflow, and marketing knowledge base.		 Build a simple Marketing AI Assistant.
9 Future of AI Marketing & AI Governance
Multimodal AI, autonomous marketing, AR/VR marketing, AI ethics, privacy, bias, copyright, hallucination, and explainable AI.		 Evaluate AI risks in marketing use cases.
Phase 4: Capstone — End-to-End AI Marketing Solution
10 Final Capstone Mini Project
Participants complete and present an AI-powered marketing solution.		 Develop, test, document, and present a portfolio-ready mini project.

📊 Teaching Method

Activity Percentage Description
Interactive Lecture 30% Concept explanation, real Indonesian marketing examples, and guided discussion.
Live Demonstration 20% Instructor demonstrates Python, ML workflow, prompt design, and RAG workflow.
Guided Practice 25% Participants follow structured exercises with instructor support.
Group Discussion 10% Use-case discussion, business interpretation, and peer feedback.

Project-Based Learning / Capstone Development 5% In-course project guidance and iterative development of the final capstone.
Clarification: The project item in the Teaching Method refers to the learning activity and development process. The Final Capstone Project in the Assessment section refers to the graded final deliverable.
